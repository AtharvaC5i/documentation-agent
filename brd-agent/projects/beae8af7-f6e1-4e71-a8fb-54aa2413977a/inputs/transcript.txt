📄 1. MEETING TRANSCRIPT

Project: Education Platform (Education4All)
Client: Tom Anderson
Team Members: Ramesh, Suresh
Date: [Insert Date]
Meeting Type: Requirement Gathering

Transcript

Tom Anderson (Client):
Thank you for joining. The goal is to build an online education platform similar to platforms like Coursera and Udemy, but more inclusive and accessible for all age groups.

Ramesh (Team):
Can you elaborate on the target audience?

Tom Anderson:
We want to cater to:

School students
College students
Working professionals
Elderly learners

The platform should be easy to use for beginners as well.

Suresh (Team):
What kind of courses should be supported?

Tom Anderson:
A wide variety:

Academic subjects
Professional skills (coding, business, etc.)
Hobby-based learning (music, art, etc.)

Ramesh:
Do you want instructors to upload their own courses?

Tom Anderson:
Yes, definitely. We need:

Instructor registration
Course creation tools
Video upload capability

Suresh:
What about user features?

Tom Anderson:
Users should be able to:

Browse and search courses
Enroll in courses
Track progress
Receive certificates after completion

Ramesh:
Should there be paid courses?

Tom Anderson:
Yes, both:

Free courses
Paid courses with secure payment integration

Suresh:
Any specific requirements for UI/UX?

Tom Anderson:
Yes:

Simple and intuitive interface
Mobile-friendly design
Personalized recommendations

Ramesh:
Do you need admin control?

Tom Anderson:
Absolutely. Admin should:

Manage users and instructors
Approve/reject courses
Monitor platform activity

Suresh:
Any analytics requirements?

Tom Anderson:
Yes, dashboards for:

User engagement
Course performance
Revenue tracking

Ramesh:
What about scalability?

Tom Anderson:
The platform should scale to thousands of users without performance issues.

Meeting Conclusion:

Build a scalable, user-friendly education platform
Support multi-role system (Admin, Instructor, Learner)
Include course management, payments, and analytics
